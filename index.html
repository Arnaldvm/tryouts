THE GAME

Starcraft Commandos is a mod of one of the best RTS of all times, Starcraft. This mod is centred around the terran unit ghost, we will create a new level and new abilities in order to make the definitive steatlh mission on Starcraft.

In Starcraft Commandos you will command a elite squad of terrans formed by marines, firebats, a medic and the ghost. Every unit is necessary in this mission, so you can't just go ahead without a plan. You have to investigate the terrain and find the best way to advance.

We will use a lot of new mechanics of other classics of strategy in PC, like Commandos: Behind Enemy Lines (1998). The enemy units will have a cone of vision and they will alert other enemies if they catch you. We will incorporate also new abilities for the units.

At the bottom of the page you are able to watch the trailer of the game and also a full gameplay, we hope you enjoy them!
